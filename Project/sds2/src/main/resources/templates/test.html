Reims: Champagne Tasting with Lunch Day Trip from Paris

Join a memorable Champagne adventure from Paris. Your friendly, English-speaking guide will start the day with a croissant breakfast right from the hotel. As you travel, the guide will share interesting stories about the Champagne region and its vineyards. The adventure begins in the cellars of renowned Champagne producers like Moët & Chandon, Mumm, or Lanson. Learn about their histories and taste their famous cuvées (depending on availability). Next up, enjoy a tasting session in the beautiful vineyards, guided by a knowledgeable wine expert. Uncover the flavors of a special cuvée and appreciate the nuances that make Champagne so delightful. At noon, a traditional French lunch awaits at a family winery or local eatery, featuring three distinct Champagnes to tickle your taste buds. The day wraps up at a cooperative winery with the final Champagne tastings. After that, the guide will smoothly navigate the way back to Paris.

Price: 292.5 EUR
